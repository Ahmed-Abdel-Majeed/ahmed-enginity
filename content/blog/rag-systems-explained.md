# RAG Systems Explained: Build Your Company's AI Brain

RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation) is the most powerful pattern in enterprise AI today. Instead of relying on a model's training data, RAG lets you ground AI responses in *your actual business data*.

## Why RAG Changes Everything

Standard ChatGPT doesn't know:
- Your product catalog
- Your company policies  
- Your past client interactions
- Your internal documentation

RAG fixes this by connecting the AI to a real-time knowledge base.

## The Architecture

A RAG system has three layers:

1. **Ingestion** — Convert your documents to vector embeddings
2. **Retrieval** — Find relevant chunks when a question is asked
3. **Generation** — Use retrieved context to generate accurate answers
